Important Tips for House Power Washing

‚úì Benefits of Professional House Washing

Removes harmful algae, mildew, and mold that damage siding and paint. Restores curb appeal and increases property value by 5-10%. Extends siding lifespan by removing damaging buildup. Prepares surfaces for painting or staining projects. Protects family health by eliminating allergens and pollutants. Maintains manufacturer warranties requiring regular cleaning. Prevents costly siding replacement from neglected maintenance.

‚ö† House Washing Safety Warnings

Never use excessive pressure on vinyl siding (can cause cracking or water infiltration). Avoid pressure washing painted wood (may strip paint). Don't clean during extreme temperatures (freezing or high heat). Keep pressure away from windows, electrical outlets, and vents. Never point spray upward under siding (forces water behind walls). Avoid mixing cleaning chemicals without professional knowledge. Always verify contractor insurance before allowing house washing (you're liable for uninsured damage).

‚Ñπ House Washing Frequency Recommendations

Myrtle Beach coastal homes typically need annual house washing due to salt air, humidity, and rapid algae growth. North-facing walls may need bi-annual cleaning (less sun exposure encourages mildew). Homes near trees or water should wash every 6-12 months. Severe algae or mildew growth requires immediate professional cleaning. Schedule washing before painting or selling your home. Most companies recommend spring cleaning (March-May) for optimal results and pricing.
